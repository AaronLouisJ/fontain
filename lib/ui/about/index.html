fontain a font-collection-system and a font-collection.

fontain was developed to:

  1. maintain a selection of free/libre/open source fonts together with their source files
  2. provide a simple solution to derive necessary formats e.g. webfonts and files for use with LaTeX.

This collection is made possible by the libre font movement.
Libre as free. Free to use, free to study, free to modify.
Libre as in freedom, not price.

You can browse the selection here or set up your own instance.
For any requests or questions don't hesitate to contact us.

fontain is inspired by Use&Modify, respectively ofont.
